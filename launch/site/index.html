Questions, answered straight

Is the free tier actually useful?

Yes — it's the entire app running in your browser with a deterministic offline engine. You can build and export a real website, generate content, and run the full Brain without paying anything or creating an account. Adding your own AI API key upgrades the writing quality; it's optional.

Where does my data live?

On the free tier, entirely on your device — we never see it. Integration API keys you add are stored in your workspace only, never echoed back to any client, and sent nowhere except the service you connected them to.

What does Pro actually add?

The managed layer: AI included (no key setup), hosted websites on your domain, and live external data (URL audits, market feeds). The core product is identical — Pro removes the setup work.

Can I cancel or leave?

Anytime. Export your whole workspace as JSON and every website as a deploy-ready bundle. HELIX is built to have zero lock-in.
